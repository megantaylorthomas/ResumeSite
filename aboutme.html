About Me

Hello and Welcome!

I love growing food, learning ways to consciously landscape, and supporting local pollinators. During the Minnesota winters, I focus that energy on my ever-growing collection of house plants. To learn more about my house plants check out my web conservatory. Another place you can frequently find me is playing with my puppy Watson!
